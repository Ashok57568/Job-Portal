FAQs

  • How do I create a job listing?

    To create a job listing, log in to your company account and navigate to the dashboard. Click on the "Create Job" button and fill in the required details such as job title, description, qualifications, and location.

  • Can I edit my job listing after posting?

    Yes, you can edit your job listing after posting. Simply go to the dashboard, locate the job listing you want to edit, and click on the "Edit" button. Make the necessary changes and save your updates.
